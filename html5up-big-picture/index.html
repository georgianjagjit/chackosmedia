Myself,
I am Jose P Chacko, a passionate filmmaking enthusiast. Born in1994, in Kasaragod, Kerala, India. I have been staying in Canada since 2017. The movie was in my blood, but I turned to hotel management. Even after working as a chef for a long time, I could not give up my passion. Eventually I decided to become a professional cinematographer. I have been making some budgetary short films with limited resources since 2019 such as no time to stop, Stay safe, Isolated View, Falsehood. I am ambitious to become a Professional filmmaker
